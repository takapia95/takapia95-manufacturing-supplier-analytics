# Manufacturing Supplier Intelligence Platform

## End-to-End Supplier Quality Analytics & Business Intelligence System

---

# Project Overview

The **Manufacturing Supplier Intelligence Platform** is a comprehensive Business Intelligence (BI) and Manufacturing Analytics project that simulates an enterprise-level supplier quality management system used by automotive manufacturers.

The platform integrates relational database design, Python-based ETL, SQL analytics, and Power BI dashboards to monitor supplier performance, manufacturing quality, delivery reliability, and corrective action management.

Instead of analyzing a single dataset, this project models the complete supplier quality lifecycle—from purchase orders and deliveries through inspections, defects, audits, and executive reporting—providing realistic operational insights for manufacturing organizations.

---

# Project Objectives

The project aims to demonstrate practical experience in designing and implementing an end-to-end manufacturing analytics solution by:

* Designing a normalized relational manufacturing database.
* Building a realistic supplier quality dataset using Python.
* Developing an automated ETL pipeline for data validation and transformation.
* Writing advanced SQL queries for operational and executive reporting.
* Creating interactive Power BI dashboards for supplier performance monitoring.
* Simulating real-world manufacturing KPIs used by supplier quality organizations.
* Applying business intelligence techniques to support continuous improvement initiatives.

---

# Business Problem

Modern manufacturing organizations receive thousands of components from multiple suppliers every day.

Maintaining high product quality requires continuous monitoring of:

* Supplier quality performance
* Incoming inspection results
* Production defects
* Delivery reliability
* Supplier audits
* Corrective actions
* Cost of Poor Quality (COPQ)

Many organizations still rely on manually generated spreadsheets and disconnected reports, making it difficult to quickly identify high-risk suppliers or emerging quality trends.

This project demonstrates how an integrated analytics platform can centralize supplier quality information and support faster, data-driven decision making.

---

# Business Questions

The platform is designed to answer operational questions such as:

* Which suppliers consistently generate the highest defect rates?
* Which suppliers have declining quality performance?
* Which manufacturing plants experience the highest rejection rates?
* Which defect categories contribute the greatest financial impact?
* Which suppliers require immediate corrective action?
* Which suppliers consistently deliver late?
* Which parts experience recurring quality issues?
* Which production lines generate the highest scrap rates?
* Which suppliers present the highest operational risk?
